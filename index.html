FAQ

Why do only four types of fuel tanks appear?

Only the smallest tank for each fuel-mass ratio is considered, larger ones are just multiples of those. The cost does consider using larger tanks when possible.

Why shutdown engines in flight?

Low Isp engines can be turned off once enough fuel has burned that the thrust-weight ratio can stay above the minimum without them and that saves fuel.

Why won't max engines go above eight?

The search time gets a lot longer as max engines increases, and for most single stage crafts too many engines won't be useful.

Why is there a warning for bodies with atmosphere?

The algorithm assumes the atmosphere is uniform for the entire flight, which it's not. Use results for bodies with atmosphere at your Kerbals' risk.
